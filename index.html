How We Work

A Clear Process.
Zero Surprises.

Every Caputo project follows a proven four-phase process designed to keep you informed, on schedule, and confident from day one.

01

Consultation

We meet on-site to understand your goals, budget, timeline, and vision. No commitment required — just a conversation.

  • Site walk & assessment
  • Budget discussion
  • Timeline expectations
02

Planning & Design

Our team produces a detailed project plan with line-item estimates, material selections, and a firm schedule.

  • Detailed cost estimate
  • Material & finish selection
  • Permit applications
03

Construction

Our crews execute with precision. You receive weekly progress updates, and our project manager is always reachable.

  • Weekly progress reports
  • Daily site supervision
  • Quality control checkpoints
04

Completion & Handoff

Final walkthrough, punch list completion, warranty documentation, and a handoff you can feel proud of.

  • Final inspection & walkthrough
  • Warranty documentation
  • 1-year follow-up service
